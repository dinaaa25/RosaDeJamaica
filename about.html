Our Story: The Red Gold of Guatemala

We are a couple—one from Europe, one from Guatemala—connected by a discovery we couldn't keep to ourselves.

In Guatemala, Rosa de Jamaica is legendary. It’s the drink you find on every corner, the local "cool-down" that’s as common as soda but entirely natural. When we first shared it together, it was a revelation: how could something this beautiful, this healthy, and this bold still be a secret in Europe?

We decided it was time to change that.

More than just a flower. It’s the ultimate lifestyle hack for anyone who loves flavor without the fluff. It’s your morning ritual as a warm, soul-soothing tea; your summer afternoon as a sharp, iced lemonade; and your weekend vibe in a craft cocktail.

From artisanal syrups to small-batch ice cream, its tart, cranberry-like edge is unlike anything else. We’re bringing this Guatemalan staple to the European stage. It’s vibrant, it’s refreshing, and it’s about to become your new favorite obsession.
